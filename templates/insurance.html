Insurances

Life insurance

To ensure that your immediate family has some financial support in the event of your demise. To finance your children's education and other needs.

Health Insurance

Health insurance provides people with a much needed financial backup at times of medical emergencies. Health risks and uncertainties are a part of life

Motor Insurance

Motor insurance secures you and provides you coverage against all unwanted accidents that may happen with you in future.

Fire insurance

Fire Insurance is a contract between the insurance company and the person requiring the policy against the loss of his property due to a fire outbreak.

Marine insurance

Cover cargo losses, damage caused to ships, cargo vessels, terminals, and any other means of transport which carries goods from one place to another.

Liability insurance

Covers the finances of the insured person in case there are any legal liabilities faced by that person against third-party claims.

A&H insurance

Accident and Health Insurance which is also implicated as A&H Insurance which covers special policies that are provided by an employer.
