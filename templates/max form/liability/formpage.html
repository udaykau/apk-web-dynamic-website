Why is Liability Insurance important?

The reason for which the liability insurance is provided can result in financial losses and can affect the income of the person whom it affects. Hence, liability insurance insures the entity against these unwanted losses of funds. The liability insurance is offered based on the potential risk of the individual or the business.

Types of Liability Insurance:

  • Public Liability
  • Product Liability
  • Commercial General Liability
  • Director and Officers Liabilities
  • Workmen Compensation/Employee Compensation
  • Shop Package Policy
  • SME- Business Advantage Policy
  • Engineering Policy
  • Office Package Policy

Benefits of Liability Insurance:

  • It covers any of the accidental injury caused to the person who is insured
  • It covers any type of damage caused to any other person or property
  • Protect the insured person against any legal activity or claims
  • It helps in minimizing the risk cost
